English translation of the Ilia State University GPA rule

GPA is calculated as the sum of each course's Grade Point (GP) multiplied by the course credits, divided by the total number of credits.

Formula: GPA = Σ(each course GP × each course credits) / Σ(total course credits).

What is GP?

GP means Grade Point. It is the converted value of a course score on a 4-point scale.
